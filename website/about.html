The Remarkable Story of the Company

For more than 120 years we have been delivering world class contraction and we've built many lasting
relationship along the way. we've matured into an industry leader and trusted resurce for those seeking
quality, innovation and reliability when building in the Denmark.

We Will Provide You The Best World which You Dreamt For!

Founded in 1901 by the visionary and pioneering Bob Henrik Hanson, the company's story began as a humble venture with a single mission: to construct dreams, one project at a time. With a keen eye for detail and an unwavering commitment to quality, he set the foundation for what would become a construction legacy. In the early years, the company took on a wide range of projects, from small residential builds to larger commercial endeavors, each executed with precision and craftsmanship.

Fast forward to the present day, and the mantle of leadership has been passed to the capable hands of Bob Henrik Hanson's grandson, also fittingly named Bob Hanson. Bob The Builders company is determined to excel from the limits and boundaries in industry of construction in order to give all the people the change to experience the serene life acquired from excellent quality of constructed buildings, houses, roads and premises.
